What is TDS?

Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) is collected by the payer before making payments like salary, commission, rent, or professional fees.

Form 16/16A

The deductor must issue a TDS certificate (Form 16 for salary, Form 16A for others) as proof of tax deduction.

Rates

Rates vary by section: 10% for professional fees (194J), 1-2% for contractors (194C), 5% for rent >50k (194IB).
