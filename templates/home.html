FAQ

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption. Your financial information is accessible only by you, and we never sell your data to any third party. Your security is our #1 priority.

Is FinTelligent really free for students?

Yes! We have a "Free Forever" plan with all the core features designed specifically for students. It helps you manage pocket money, budget for goals, and track expenses without spending a dime.

Do I have to link my bank accounts?

No, linking accounts is entirely optional. You can manually log your expenses easily. Linked accounts simply make automatic tracking fully seamless if you choose to enable it.

Can my parents or college see my spending?

Not at all. Your account is completely private and personal. No one else has access to your budget or spending habits unless you explicitly choose to share them.
