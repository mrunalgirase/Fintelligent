Internship & Stipend: The Tax Lowdown

Is my stipend taxable?

Yes, technically. But if your total annual income is below ₹7.5 Lakhs (New Regime), you owe **Zero Tax**. However, companies might still deduct 10% TDS under Section 194J.

How to get my TDS back?

File your Income Tax Return (ITR-1 or ITR-4) next July. The "refund" will be credited directly to your bank account. It's like a forced savings account! 💸

The 194J vs 192 Hack

If you're an intern, you're often treated as a "Professional" (194J - 10% TDS) rather than an "Employee" (192 - Slab rates). Always check your offer letter!
